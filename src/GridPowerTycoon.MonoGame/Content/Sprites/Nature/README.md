# Nature Pixel Art Prototype

Milestone 29B introduces the first 32x32 nature prototype assets.

These files are source PNGs and are intentionally simple. They are not final art and are not wired into the renderer yet. Their purpose is to establish the first concrete direction for natural obstacles/resources.

Current families:

| Family | Files | Role |
|---|---:|---|
| forest | 4 | dense tree/resource tile variants |
| mountain | 3 | rocky obstacle/resource tile variants |

Forests and mountains remain tile-sized prototypes for now. If later art needs larger silhouettes, the renderer should still preserve a 32x32 footprint contract unless explicit metadata says otherwise.
